A Song of Ice and Fire? I Thought it Was Called Game of Thrones!

With the explosive popularity of the HBO t.v. series Game of Thrones, a lot of confusion has come around over which titles to use
to reference the TV show or the book series. I personally prefer to use Game of Thrones for the show, and A Song of Ice and Fire for the books.
The first book in the series is actually titled "A Game of Thrones", which is where the show runners, David Benioff and D.B. Weiss, got the show title from.
